FAQ'S

How do I schedule a pet grooming appointment?

To schedule a pet grooming appointment, simply visit our website and click on the "Book Grooming" button. Follow the prompts to choose your preferred date and time, and our team will take care of the rest.

What services do you offer for pet daycare?

We offer a range of services for pet daycare, including supervised playtime, exercise, socialization, and feeding. Your pet will have a fun and safe day in our care.

Can I book a pet walking service on a recurring basis?

Yes, you can! We offer flexible scheduling options, including daily, weekly, or custom recurring pet walking services to fit your needs and your pet's routine.

What does your pet massage and spa service include?

Our pet massage and spa service includes gentle massages to relax and soothe your pet, along with spa treatments like baths, grooming, and nail trims to keep them looking and feeling their best.

How can I enroll my pet in your pet training programs?

Enrolling your pet in our training programs is easy. Just contact our team, and we'll help you choose the right program for your pet's needs and schedule a training session.

What are the accommodations like for pet boarding?

Our pet boarding facilities provide a comfortable and safe environment for your pet. Each pet has its own spacious enclosure, regular exercise, and social interaction with our trained staff.

Are your pet care professionals certified and trained?

Yes, our pet care professionals are highly trained and certified in their respective fields. We prioritize the well-being and safety of your pets, and our team is committed to providing the best care possible.

How do you handle emergencies or medical issues for pets in your care?

Our staff is trained to recognize and respond to emergencies. In the event of a medical issue, we have protocols in place to ensure your pet receives prompt attention, and we will contact you immediately.

Can I tour your facilities before booking a service for my pet?

Absolutely! We encourage you to schedule a tour of our facilities to see where your pet will be staying and meet our team. Just give us a call to arrange a visit.

What are your hours of operation for pet services?

Our hours of operation vary by service and location. You can find our service hours on our website, or feel free to contact us for specific details about our operating hours for each service.

Didn't find any Solution?

If you didn't find a solution to your query, we're here to assist you!

Feel free to reach out to us via live chat or by using the contact form at home page.
