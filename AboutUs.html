Welcome to Quby Pet - Your Trusted Pet Management Partner.

About Us

In today's society, pets are no longer just animals; they have become cherished family members. Quby Pet, founded in 2022 by passionate animal lovers, is dedicated to improving the lives of pets and their owners.

We not only provide pet services, including pet grooming, pet day care, pet walking, pet massage and spa, pet training and pet boarding, but also sell pet products.Our experienced team is ready to care for your pet seven days a week in Ipoh. You can find us conveniently located in Ipoh Parade.

Why Choose Us?

Experienced Professionals:
Our team, comprised of experienced veterinarians, skilled pet groomers, and dedicated pet care specialists, ensures your pet receives the highest standard of care and attention.
Convenient Appointments:
Our user-friendly online booking system simplifies scheduling pet grooming or veterinary check-up appointments, allowing you to schedule your pet's appointments at your convenience.
Safe and Comfortable Environment:
Our pet boarding facility offers a safe, clean, and comfortable home for your pets, providing love, playtime, and attention during their stay.
Personalized Care:
We cater to your pet's unique needs by tailoring our services, including special diets, medication, and grooming preferences, to ensure a personalized experience.
